# GrabIT

Most map apps answer the question, "What's popular here?" GrabIT answers the one that actually matters: "What should you do right now?"

GrabIT, pronounced "Grab it," is a personalized travel discovery assistant for tourists and travelers exploring unfamiliar places. Instead of showing generic map results, it helps users interact with a map and get hyper-local recommendations based on their saved interests, current location, and travel context.

Built with GrabMaps APIs, GrabIT uses keyword search, nearby place discovery, and directions to surface places that are not just popular, but personally relevant. The goal is simple: help people discover the right place at the right moment.

## Why GrabIT

When people travel, better navigation is not enough. The real challenge is discovering places that match their vibe, interests, and situation in the moment.

Traditional map apps are great at helping users get from point A to point B, but they are still built around generic search. GrabIT is designed for discovery. It focuses on what is best for this user, in this place, right now.

## Who It Is For

GrabIT is built for:

- Tourists visiting unfamiliar cities
- Travelers who want spontaneous, personalized recommendations
- Users who want to discover places beyond generic top-rated results

## What We Built

GrabIT combines personalization, location awareness, and live map intelligence into one experience:

- Personalized discovery based on interests saved during signup
- Hyper-local recommendations around the user's current location
- GrabMaps-powered search, nearby discovery, and directions
- Bumblebee alerts that send instant Telegram notifications when relevant places come within range

With Bumblebee turned on, users get notified about places they actually care about, whether that is a hidden cafe matching their vibe, a nearby activity aligned with their interests, or a spot they might have otherwise missed.

## What Judges Should Pay Attention To

- Personalization: results are tailored to each user's saved interests, not just popularity
- Hyper-local context: recommendations are based on the user's live location and what is relevant nearby
- Bumblebee experience: Telegram alerts turn the map into a hands-free travel companion

## Why It Stands Out

Google Maps and other traditional map apps are excellent for navigation, but they are still optimized for broad, generic discovery. GrabIT is better for this use case because it personalizes recommendations around the user, not the average traveler.

Instead of asking users to search harder, filter more, and guess what might be worth visiting, GrabIT helps answer a much more useful question in real time: what should I do right now?
